Registration Info for 2016-17

Students pursuing the BS in Computer Science should note that CSCI 3372 is offered in the Spring of 2017 and not is offered in the Spring of 2018.
BS students graduating in 2017 or 2018 who have not taken CSCI 3372 should plan to take it during the spring of 2017.

Students pursuing the BS in Computer Science should note that CSCI 2201 can satisfy the requirement for Computer Ethics.
