BC CS Honors Program

Computer Science majors of junior or senior standing with at least a 3.3 grade point average in CS courses are eligible to join the Departmental Honors Program. In order to graduate with the Departmental Honors designation, the student must:

  • maintain at least a 3.3 grade point average in CS courses
  • complete a senior thesis. In order to complete a senior thesis, an eligible student must have a thesis proposal approved by a faculty advisor and by the Honors committee by the end of their junior year
  • complete two sections of CSCI4961 Honors Thesis during their senior year
  • submit a written honors thesis by the last day of class in the second semester of their senior year
  • make an oral presentation of their thesis at end of their senior year
